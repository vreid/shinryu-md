# Bae Myunghi

Born: February 17, 1970 — Tongyeong, South Korea

Recruited through Ryu BioPharma's security division in late 2000. She was
low-ranking staff at the Ryu compounds in Incheon—the kind of person who quietly
made sure things ran correctly without being asked. Ryu Ji-seok noticed that the
areas she oversaw never had problems, asked around, and assigned her to Caspar
personally. No interview. She was told to pack.

Tall, broad-shouldered, permed hair. Irons creases into school trousers because
a man without a crease lacks self-respect. Loyal to Caspar first.

Her cooking is better than Jungsook's, a source of quiet permanent war.
