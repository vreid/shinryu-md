# Moon Jungsook

Born: October 3, 1968 — Daegu, South Korea

Selected by Shin Hee-yeon from the Shin Entertainment domestic staff in 2000,
when Hee-yeon was pregnant and dissatisfied with every candidate the agencies
sent. Jungsook was managing the Shin household's Seoul residence—impossibly
young for the role, impossibly good at it. Hee-yeon saw a woman who could run a
schedule, read a room, and never once flatter. Hired on the spot.

Compact, center-parted bob. Korean in her cooking and standards, European in her
pragmatism. Inspects produce with visible disapproval. Loyal to Isabelle first,
the families second.

Her cooking is rivaled only by Myunghi's—a source of quiet permanent war between
them.
